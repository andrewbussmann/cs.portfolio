My Vacations

I have been on a lot of vacations, but I will share my favorites.

My third favorite vacation was Mexico in 2016 spring break. We only went there for less than a week, but while we were there, it was paradise. We stayed in a hotel with a private beach, and we did a lot of snorkling there. Unfortunately, I had pneumonia while we were there, but it was still amazing. I will share two photos below, one from the beautiful Mayan Ruins of Tulum that we visited while there, and one of my family snorkling in a beautiful cave.

My second favorite vacation that I have been on is Costa Rica in 2014. We stayed there with my mom, dad, brother, grandma, and grandpa. We went to many different places, and I loved all of them. The best part about this vacation was seeing all of the cool animals. We went on many hikes while we were there, one of which was to the top of the highest mountain in Costa Rica. This was a very fun hike, except for the ground bees that kept flying at me because of my neon shirt. I will share my two favorite pictures of the animals below. The first picture is called a quetzal. These are extremely rare birds, but we were fortunate enough to see many of them. The second picture is of a squirrel monkey. We saw four types of monkeys (howler, squirrel, spider, and white-faced) but these are the cutest ones.

My all-time favorite vacation was Europe 2016. We visited Iceland, Germany, and Austria. My favorite was Germany, because we spent the most time there. This was an amazing vacation with me, my mom, my dad, my brother, my grandma, my grandpa, my aunt, my uncle, and my cousins. I will share one picture from every country below. Order: Germany, Iceland, Austria for pictures. The first picture is of me standing in front of a castle that we stayed in in Germany. This castle was so cool to stay in, and I loved walking around the castle grounds. The second picture is of the ocean off of the coast of Iceland. We were only there for one day, but the scenery there was amazing. The final picture that I have below is of The Alps in Austria. We stayed in a city called Lofer and it was amazing there. I loved the fog that would cover the mountains in the morning, and how the mountains were so clear in the afternoon.
